About KJ6ER

I have been licensed since 1987 and upgraded to Amateur Extra in 1991. I received my B.S. Electrical Engineering degree at Case Institute of Technology in Cleveland and worked in Silicon Valley technology companies for 45 years after graduation. I recently retired to design portable HF antennas and share them with the worldwide amateur radio community for free as my way of giving back to this hobby I love.

My mission is to design efficient and effective portable antennas that integrate performance (high radiation efficiency), elegance (speed and simplicity of deployment), and convenience (ease of transport and storage). These designs are always shared for free with the global amateur radio community to encourage more operators to homebrew their own.

Always feel free to reach out to me anytime with questions and comments, I'd be happy to help. Keep having fun out there, 73!
