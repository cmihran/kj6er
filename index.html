About Me

I'm Greg Mihran — KJ6ER, writing from Silicon Valley. I've been a licensed amateur since 1987 and now spend most of my time designing portable antennas for the every-ham.

My mission is to design efficient and effective portable antennas focused on the optimal integration of performance (high radiation efficiency), elegance (speed and simplicity of deployment), and convenience (ease of transport and storage). These designs are always shared for free with the global amateur radio community to encourage more operators to homebrew their own portable antennas.

Building ham radio antennas is our last homebrewing frontier, and I'm dedicated to helping you do so successfully. As always, feel free to reach out if you ever have any questions — I'd be happy to help. Keep having fun out there! 73.

Heroes & Beta Testers

Two ways to get more involved in the KJ6ER antenna community.

KJ6ER Antenna Heroes

Show us how you build and deploy your KJ6ER antennas. Send me a link to a video — portable or at your home QTH — and I'll feature it in an upcoming newsletter. I know many of these videos already exist; I want to offer a global platform for the creative builders out there to share their tips and tricks.

KJ6ER Antenna Beta Testers

Want early access to new designs? Beta testers receive draft PDFs ahead of release, build and deploy them, and summarize their experience on video. Your feedback helps me improve the PDF, the design, and the deployment process. Three new antennas are coming in 2026 — I'd appreciate your help.

To join either program, send me a message.
