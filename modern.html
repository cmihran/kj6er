About

About KJ6ER

Hi, I'm KJ6ER. I've been licensed since the 1970s and I build wire antennas in my backyard. This site is where I share what works, what doesn't, and the plans I've drawn up for other hams who want to put more signal out with less money.

All plans here are free. If you build one, I'd love to hear about it — drop me a line or join the mailing list and I'll send a note when I post something new.
