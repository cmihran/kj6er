About

About KJ6ER

I'm Greg Mihran — KJ6ER. I've been a licensed amateur since 1987 and now spend most of my time designing portable vertical antennas for POTA, SOTA, and emergency ops. After forty-five years in Silicon Valley tech, I retired in 2022 and went all-in on the hobby.

Everything on this site is open: I draw up the plans, model them in 4NEC2, build and field-test them, and post the PDFs for free. They're living documents — I tweak them as I learn more. If you build one, I'd love to hear how it worked out. 73.
